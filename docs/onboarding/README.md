## Shared Structure

Each pack includes:

- first-day setup
- first-week learning path
- role readiness checklist
- daily and weekly operating habits
- KPI guide
- 30-day signoff prompts

## Suggested Use By Team Leads

1. Share the role SOP first.
2. Walk the new hire through the relevant onboarding pack.
3. Pair them with a seeded demo workflow in the app.
4. Review readiness at the end of week one.
5. Review KPI quality and operating discipline at day 30.
