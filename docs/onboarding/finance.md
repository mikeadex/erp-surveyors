# Finance Onboarding Pack

This guide is for finance staff responsible for invoice lifecycle management, receivables tracking, and finance dashboard monitoring.

## First-Day Setup

- confirm web login works
- open:
  - dashboard
  - invoices
  - invoice detail
  - notifications
- review how case stage and invoice stage interact

## First-Week Learning Path

### Day 1

- read the [finance SOP](/Users/michaeladeleye/Documents/product/ERP Surveyors/docs/sops/finance.md)
- review the finance dashboard snapshot and invoice statuses

### Day 2

- create a draft invoice from an eligible seeded case
- edit amount, tax, due date, and notes

### Day 3

- issue an invoice in a demo flow
- observe case stage movement and notification behavior

### Day 4

- mark an invoice as paid in a demo flow
- review how receivables and case stage update

### Day 5

- review sent and overdue invoice queues
- practice identifying finance escalation scenarios

## Role Readiness Checklist

The team lead should confirm the person can:

- create a draft invoice correctly
- edit commercial terms safely
- issue and mark paid correctly
- recognize when voiding is appropriate
- use the finance dashboard for receivables follow-up

## Daily Habits

- review unpaid and overdue invoice queues
- update paid invoices promptly
- keep finance notes clear
- watch projection and overdue amount on the dashboard

## Weekly Habits

- review overdue invoices with management
- review invoice creation lag after final issue
- review outstanding amounts by branch or team

## KPIs To Watch

- invoice issue turnaround after final report issue
- total unpaid invoice count
- overdue invoice value
- paid-this-month and paid-this-year totals
- collection lag from issue date to payment date

## 30-Day Signoff Questions

- can the person manage the invoice lifecycle independently?
- are they keeping receivable status current?
- can they identify collection risk early?
- do they understand how finance actions affect case status and dashboards?
