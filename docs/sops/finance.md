# Finance SOP

This SOP is for finance users handling invoices, payment tracking, receivables visibility, and cash-follow-up inside the platform.

## Primary Objective

Convert completed valuation work into issued invoices, monitor payment status clearly, and keep receivables visible to leadership.

## Main Areas You Own

- draft invoice creation
- invoice issue
- payment recording
- receivables monitoring
- overdue invoice follow-up
- finance dashboard review

## Daily Workflow

1. Open the finance dashboard snapshot.
2. Review:
   - income collected
   - projected receipts
   - unpaid invoice count
   - overdue amount
3. Open the invoices register and filter by:
   - `draft`
   - `sent`
   - `overdue`
4. Create draft invoices for eligible completed cases.
5. Review commercial terms before issuing.
6. Mark paid invoices promptly so dashboards and case stages stay current.

## Invoice Rules

- one invoice per case
- invoice client and case must remain consistent
- draft invoices can be edited
- only eligible statuses should move to issue, paid, or void
- finance actions should happen inside the invoice workflow, not outside the system

## Draft Invoice Checklist

Before issuing, confirm:

- the case is the correct one
- client details are correct
- amount, tax, due date, and notes are correct
- the invoice is still in draft

## Payment Handling

When payment is received:

- mark the invoice as paid in the platform
- confirm amount and timing internally
- let the system advance the case stage to `payment_received`

## Overdue Follow-Up

Use the finance dashboard and invoice list to monitor:

- overdue invoice count
- overdue invoice value
- outstanding sent invoices

Follow up with the responsible operational or client-facing team when payment is delayed.

## Escalation Triggers

Escalate when:

- an issued invoice remains unpaid beyond due date
- an invoice was created for the wrong case or client
- a sent invoice appears to require voiding or correction
- payment has been received but not reflected in system state

## Handoffs

- from valuers/operations:
  final issued case ready for billing
- to management:
  receivable pressure, overdue exposure, and collection status
